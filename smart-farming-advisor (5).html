🟤 Black Cotton Soil
High water retention. Irrigate every 8–10 days. Risk of waterlogging — use furrow method.
🟡 Red Laterite Soil
Low retention. Irrigate every 4–5 days. Drip irrigation recommended for efficiency.
⚪ Sandy Alluvial
Very low retention. Irrigate every 2–3 days. Short bursts work better than long sessions.
🟫 Medium Clay Loam
Good balance. Irrigate every 6–7 days. Suitable for most crops. Sprinkler or drip both work.
